About OstaFandy

Where craftsmanship meets trust — from a simple idea to Egypt’s go-to home service solution.

How It All Started

OstaFandy began in 2020, born out of frustration with unreliable home repair services. Our founders — lifelong friends Ahmed Samir and Nour Khalil — experienced firsthand how hard it was to find skilled, trustworthy handymen. From their small garage office in Cairo, they launched a platform to change that.

What started as a two-person team matching local plumbers to nearby homes quickly grew into a multi-city network. Today, OstaFandy is trusted by thousands, offering scheduled repairs, emergency bookings, and professional support — all at your fingertips.
